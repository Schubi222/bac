Music and sound effects have always been a great tool to create immersion, atmosphere, convey emotions and add depth. It is no different in the video game industry. This is why there are often whole sound teams working on the production. There will be teams hunting for the best sound effects and using all their creativity to create the perfect sound effect. In order to record a soundtrack or some background music musicians will be hired sometimes a whole orchestra this will most likely include someone to compose the music.
